Our Story

Where Every Cup
Feels Like Home

Tucked in the heart of Srikakulam, Cafe Cozy was born from a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee can turn an ordinary day into something extraordinary.

We source only the finest single-origin and blended beans from the Nilgiris, Coorg, and beyond. Our baristas are craftspeople, treating every pour as a moment worth perfecting.

Whether you're catching up with a friend, diving deep into a book, or stealing a quiet moment for yourself — our doors are open, our chairs are comfortable, and the coffee is always freshly brewed.
